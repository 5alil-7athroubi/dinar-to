About Dinar-To

Dinar-To is a specialized platform created to help Tunisians make international money transfers, overcoming the limitations that prevent direct international transfers from Tunisia to other countries. Dinar-To connects users with trusted mediators, allowing safe and efficient peer-to-peer transactions facilitated by admin oversight.

How It Works

  1. Create a Request: Users submit transfer requests by specifying the amount and destination details.
  2. Admin Approval: The platform admins review each request to ensure compliance and approve or reject it accordingly.
  3. Transfer Process: Upon approval, the transaction is completed via Dinar-To network of mediators.
  4. Receive Confirmation: Both users and mediators receive transfer updates, making the process transparent and secure.

Why Choose Dinar-To?

  • Secure Transactions: All transfers are reviewed and authorized by our admin team to ensure safety and integrity.
  • Flexible Network: Dinar-To leverages a peer-to-peer model to facilitate transfers, making the process smoother and more accessible.
  • Quick & Reliable: Once approved, transfers are promptly processed, helping you reach your destination faster.
